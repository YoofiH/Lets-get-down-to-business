Free instrumental VST:

II was able to get some free VSTs, and since they're free/open source, which is good for beginners!

  • Labs: The best free VST.
  • Vital: a powerful VST synthesizer
  • Vinyl Guitar:A great guitar like VST not that many preset but its good
  • Mel-lofi: Best lofi instumental VST for brass noises i.e. trumpets, sax and more

  • Izotope Vinyl: For the static old song feeling
